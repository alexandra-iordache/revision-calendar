The Staircase Strategy

Your exams are spread over 18 days with gaps between each one. The strategy: build deep knowledge of all modules during pre-season (now until May 7), then use the gap days between exams for laser-focused cramming on the next paper. IY3612 and IY3660 are front-loaded because they're first AND hardest.

Phase Breakdown

PHASE 1

Foundation Building (Mar 24 - Apr 20) — 4 weeks

Learn all topics across all 6 modules. ~30% of time on IY3660 (starting from scratch), ~25% on IY3612 (hardest module, first exam), and remaining 45% split across IY3609, CS3003, IY5606, IY3501. IY3501 needs lighter touch since you've mostly completed it. Use Active Recall + Spaced Repetition from day one.

PHASE 2

Past Paper Intensive (Apr 21 - May 4) — 2 weeks

Timed past papers for all modules under exam conditions. Mark your own work. Identify weak spots and drill them. Still weighted toward IY3612 + IY3660 but touching all modules.

PHASE 3

Pre-Exam Sprints (May 5 - May 28)

Each gap between exams is dedicated to the NEXT exam. 2-4 days of focused revision using flashcard blitz + final past paper + weak-area targeted review. After each exam, immediately switch focus.

Active Recall Techniques

Blurting

The Blurting Method

Read a topic for 10 min. Close everything. Write EVERYTHING you remember on a blank page. Open notes — use a different colour to fill gaps. The gaps are what to focus on next.

Flashcards

Active Recall Cards

Use Anki or physical cards. Front: question. Back: answer. Rate confidence 1-5. Wrong cards return sooner. For IY3660: every security definition + every block cipher step.

Teach It

Feynman Technique

Explain a concept out loud as if teaching a friend. If you stumble, you've found a gap. Essential for IY3612 — practise saying "brief, reasoned answers" aloud.

Past Papers

Exam Simulation

In Phases 2-3, do papers under timed conditions (2hrs). Mark yourself. This is the single best predictor of exam performance.

Your Ideal Study Day (ADHD-Optimised)

Each block = 3 Pomodoros (25+5, 25+5, 25+5 = 90 min). Max 3 blocks/day (4.5 hrs focused study). Hardest module always goes in Block 1 when your brain is freshest.
08:00

Morning Routine

Wake, shower, breakfast, coffee. No screens for 15 min — protects dopamine for study ahead.

09:00

BLOCK 1 — Hardest Module (IY3612 or IY3660)

3 Pomodoros. Use Active Recall: close notes, write everything you remember, then check. Never just re-read.

10:30

Big Break (20 min)

Walk, snack, stretch. Leave your desk completely. Movement resets ADHD focus.

11:00

BLOCK 2 — Second Module (IY3609 / IY5606 / CS3003)

3 Pomodoros. Try Blurting: read topic 10 min, then write everything on blank page.

12:30

Lunch + Life Admin (75 min)

Cook a proper meal. Do one chore: laundry, dishes, tidy. You live alone — staying on top of this prevents stress spirals.

14:00

BLOCK 3 — Spaced Repetition / Past Papers

3 Pomodoros. Review flashcards from 2, 5, 7 days ago. Or do a timed past paper section.

15:30

STUDY DONE

Brain needs rest to consolidate. No guilt. You earned this.

15:30+

Free Time

Exercise, gaming, socialising, groceries, cooking, Netflix. One evening/week: batch-cook meals.

22:00

Wind Down (Optional)

10 min light flashcard skim. Reviewing before sleep boosts retention. But sleep > studying.
